About Me

Humaira Eshika is a highly celebrated Bangladeshi playback singer recognized for her versatile vocal range, rich tone, and deep passion for both traditional folk and modern melodies. Widely acclaimed across the country, she became a national household name following her stellar performances on national platforms. With an ever-growing discography and prestigious accolades, Humaira continues to enchant audiences through her dedicated artistry, expressive storytelling, and commanding stage presence.

✦ Career

Humaira's musical trajectory was set in motion at a young age, earning distinct honors such as the National Child Award in 2013 and winning top prizes at the Shishu Academy regional competitions. She transitioned flawlessly into the professional mainstream arena in 2016 as a prominent top finalist on Channel i's highly competitive music reality show, "Khude Gaanraj". Her exceptional talent culminated in industry-wide recognition when she clinched the prestigious 18th Channel i Music Award for her breakthrough contributions. Collaborating with top-tier producers and musicians, she continues to dominate charts with popular releases like "Pran Bhomra" and massive crossover projects with industry hitmakers.
