About Me

Humaira Eshika is a talented and rapidly rising vocalist in the Bangladeshi music industry, admired for her soulful voice and versatile musical style.

Early Journey

Career Highlights

Eshika's professional journey gained major momentum in 2016 when she became a finalist on Channel i's renowned reality show Khude Gaanraj, where she was honored with titles like "Voice of the Day" and "Voice of the Month". Her breakthrough hit "Tumi Chara" from the film Rakkhosh received widespread critical acclaim and millions of digital views.
